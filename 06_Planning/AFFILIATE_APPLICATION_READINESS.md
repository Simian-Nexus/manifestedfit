# Affiliate Application Readiness

## Current Reality

Manifested Fit has a live site, a lead magnet, and a resource hub, but the public audience is still early:

- YouTube channel exists but has no videos yet.
- Facebook, Instagram, X, TikTok, and other social accounts still need to be set up.
- The email capture form works as a starter CSV bridge, but no production email platform automation is connected yet.
- No approved affiliate account is in place for the aspirational main offer.

This means the immediate strategy is not "apply everywhere today." The stronger strategy is to build enough public proof that affiliate managers can see a real niche, a real funnel, a consistent content engine, and basic audience traction.

## Mindvalley Reality Check

Mindvalley is still a strong aspirational offer for this niche, but it is selective.

Official Mindvalley affiliate page states:

- applications go through Impact
- baseline commission is 30%
- cookie lifetime is 30 days
- the dashboard is hosted on Impact
- they look for a well-established audience
- they list a minimum follower/list size of 75,000 fans/followers
- they value engagement and consistent traffic
- if they feel the application is a fit, they generally respond within 7 days; no response within 15 days means unsuccessful

Sources:

- `https://www.mindvalley.com/affiliates`
- `https://www.mindvalley.com/partnerships`

## Application Thesis

The application should eventually say:

Manifested Fit is a mind-body wellness brand that helps people pair manifestation, gentle fitness, reflection, and practical daily rituals. The site offers a free interactive 7-day reset, captures email subscribers, publishes consistent short-form and Pinterest content, and recommends aligned personal-growth resources with clear affiliate disclosure.

## Evidence To Build Before Applying To Selective Programs

Minimum useful proof:

- live website at `https://manifestedfit.com` or `http://manifestedfit.com`
- resources page with clear disclosure
- at least 10 polished short-form videos or posts
- at least 20 Pinterest pins
- at least 3 channels set up with consistent branding
- at least one email platform connected or a clear subscriber-management plan
- early analytics screenshots or numbers, even if modest
- a short media kit page or PDF with niche, audience, channels, and content plan

For Mindvalley specifically, wait until there is meaningful traction unless the goal is just to learn the application flow.

## Lower-Barrier Affiliate Targets First

Before Mindvalley approval, research and apply to lower-barrier programs in adjacent categories:

- meditation and breathwork apps
- yoga, mobility, stretching, or gentle fitness programs
- habit trackers, journals, planners, and wellness printables
- sleep, stress, or recovery tools
- personal-development book/course marketplaces

Selection criteria:

- reputable product
- public affiliate terms
- reasonable approval bar
- fit with the 7-day reset
- no aggressive health, weight-loss, or income claims
- allowed promotion on organic social and Pinterest

## Success Path

1. Build the social profiles.
2. Publish a first 30-day content sprint.
3. Connect email automation.
4. Add analytics.
5. Apply to easier aligned affiliate programs.
6. Add active offers to `/resources/`.
7. Keep publishing and collecting early data.
8. Apply to Mindvalley once there is enough audience proof to be taken seriously.
