# Content System

## Weekly Rhythm

Create one theme per week, then split it into assets:

- 1 short article or landing-page section
- 5 Pinterest pins
- 2 short-form video scripts
- 1 email
- 1 lead magnet improvement or bonus

## Starter Themes

- morning reset rituals
- manifestation and movement
- energy without burnout
- gentle consistency
- body confidence without pressure
- sleep, stress, and recovery
- visualization for fitness goals
- habit stacking for wellness

## File Organization

- Pinterest: `05_Content/pinterest/`
- Email: `05_Content/email/`
- Shorts: `05_Content/video-shorts/`
- Approved media: `Media/`
- Lead magnet planning: `04_Lead_Magnets/`
