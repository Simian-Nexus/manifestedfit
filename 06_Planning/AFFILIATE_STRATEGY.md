# Affiliate Strategy

> **Status:** 🟢 Active reference — funnel, offer criteria, and compliance guardrails are still the live plan. Mindvalley remains aspirational-only (75k-follower bar, per `AFFILIATE_APPLICATION_READINESS.md`); no offers are approved yet.

## Core Offer

Initial aspirational offer: Mindvalley.

Why it fits:

- personal growth and transformation match the Manifested Fit audience
- official affiliate page lists a 30% baseline commission
- application and dashboard flow are through Impact
- strong product library for mindset, wellness, meditation, productivity, and personal development

Important caveat:

Mindvalley's official affiliate page says they look for a well-established list or following. Build the site, list, and traffic now, but do not let launch depend on Mindvalley approval.

## Funnel

Traffic source:

- Pinterest first, then short-form video repurposing

Landing page:

- `Manifested Fit` brand promise
- 7-day mind-body reset opt-in
- no hard affiliate pitch before trust is earned

Lead magnet:

- interactive daily reset planner
- small habit loop: intention, movement, nourishment, reflection
- contextual affiliate CTA after value is delivered

Email sequence:

- deliver the lead magnet
- reinforce daily ritual
- share personal or editorial story
- introduce the affiliate product as a deeper path
- invite a click with transparent disclosure

Affiliate tracking:

- use UTM tags on Pinterest pins
- keep public affiliate links in `03_Website/public/assets/js/affiliate-offers.js`
- later add redirect slugs such as `/go/mindvalley/`

## Fallback Offer Categories

If Mindvalley approval is delayed, look for offers in:

- meditation apps
- yoga or mobility programs
- habit trackers and journals
- wellness courses
- health-focused digital memberships

Approval criteria:

- credible product
- clear commission terms
- conversion assets available
- no exaggerated health or income claims
- audience fit with Manifested Fit

## Compliance Guardrails

- Put an affiliate disclosure near affiliate CTAs.
- Do not imply guaranteed fitness, health, weight loss, or income outcomes.
- Treat the website as educational and inspirational, not medical advice.
- Use a real unsubscribe-capable email platform before sending ongoing campaigns at scale.
