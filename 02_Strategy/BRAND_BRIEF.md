# Manifested Fit Brand Brief

## Positioning

Manifested Fit helps people combine gentle wellness habits, mindset, and embodied goal-setting so fitness feels aligned instead of forced.

## Audience

Primary audience:

- people drawn to manifestation, wellness, yoga, meditation, and self-improvement
- beginners who want better energy, confidence, and consistency without harsh fitness culture
- people likely to save/share visual content on Pinterest and short-form platforms

## Brand Feel

- calm, grounded, optimistic
- practical enough to trust
- spiritual without becoming vague
- fitness-positive without shame, pressure, or extreme transformation claims

## Visual Direction

Use the existing teal, green, and purple logo as the anchor. Pair it with warm off-white, leaf green, deep teal, and restrained plum accents.

Avoid:

- fake medical certainty
- exaggerated before/after promises
- overly dark or one-note purple layouts
- generic stock wellness language

## Funnel Promise

The first lead magnet promise:

> Build a small daily mind-body ritual that connects intention, movement, nourishment, and reflection.
